LED Monitor

  • ECE 153B Project

Project Overview

For our project, we have implemented a LED monitor device which changes its brightness depending on measurements of distance or temperature. The user will specify whether the device should take a measurement for distance or temperature and the device will change its brightness according to the measurement. For higher temperatures and longer distances the brightness of the LED will be brighter. For lower temperatures and smaller distances the brightness of the LED will be lower.
